Dental Exams

a routine check-up where a dentist examines your teeth, gums, and mouth to make
sure everything is healthy. They look for problems like cavities, gum disease, or other oral health
issues. Sometimes, they take X-rays to see areas not visible to the eye. Regular dental exams help
prevent problems and keep your mouth in good shape!
